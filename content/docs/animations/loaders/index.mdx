## Overview

Loader components for showing loading states, progress indicators, and placeholder UI. Each loader is highly customizable with props for size, color, duration, and more.

## Available Loaders

### Spinners & Circular
- **Spinner** - Classic circular spinning loader
- **Pulse** - Pulsing circle with scale and opacity
- **Orbit** - Particles orbiting in a circle
- **Morphing** - Shape that morphs between different forms

### Animated Elements
- **Dots** - Bouncing dots in sequence
- **Bars** - Animated bars with varying heights
- **Gooey Blobs** - Blobs with merging gooey effect

### Progress & Placeholders
- **Liquid Progress** - Liquid-style progress bar
- **Skeleton** - Shimmer effect placeholder
